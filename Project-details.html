PROJECT OVERVIEW

JOVANA LOUIS is a modern luxury jewelry brand crafted for timeless elegance and understated sophistication. The identity blends classic refinement with a contemporary aesthetic, designed to feel premium, graceful, and enduring. The logo features a minimal monogram symbol paired with an elegant serif wordmark. The emblem represents exclusivity and craftsmanship, while the refined typography communicates trust, heritage, and high-end appeal. A deep emerald green backdrop combined with soft gold tones reinforces the brand’s luxurious character, evoking prestige, confidence, and timeless beauty. This identity is designed to scale seamlessly across packaging, jewelry boxes, certificates, storefronts, social media, and digital platforms—maintaining a consistent and recognizable presence across all touchpoints.
